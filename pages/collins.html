The challenge


Flight deck design is incredibly complex. Our users are pilots who need tools that are intuitive, reliable, and able to support quick decision-making in high-stakes environments.


Beyond usability, there are strict aerospace regulations and technical constraints we must work within, along with the need to present highly technical information in ways that are instantly understandable.


Another challenge is communication: translating design ideas into a language that pilots, engineers, and executives from very different backgrounds can all easily grasp.
