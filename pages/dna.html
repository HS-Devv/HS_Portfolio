VRAC

VRAC (formerly the Virtual Reality Applications Center) at ISU is the home of the human computer interaction major and a wide variety of research projects. At VRAC I participated in several projects, namely the designing user interfaces for the Ship Dimensional Analysis Tool (SDAT) project as well as for an educational game called Origami Jump.

Origami Jump

Origami Jump is an educational videogame that teaches students about the concept of DNA origami, the process for folding and manipulating DNA nucleotides into specific shapes.



My main role on the team was to design a user interface for the player that facilitated the player finding collectible nucleotides and using them to fold the level geometry and solve various origami puzzles.
